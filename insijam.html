Why Accessibility Matters?

Over 1 billion people worldwide live with disabilities and rely on accessible digital experiences. Most existing websites were never designed for accessibility, exposing organizations to compliance risks, lost users, and poor digital inclusivity.

Core Features

Visual Accessibility

Font resizing, spacing controls, dyslexia-friendly fonts, high contrast mode, dark mode, grayscale, and color muting.

Reading Support

Reading guides, focus mode, reading masks, and cognitive-friendly layouts for distraction-free experiences.

Navigation & Motor

Full keyboard navigation, skip-to-content shortcuts, visible focus indicators, and enhanced cursor support.

Audio & Structure

Arabic & English text-to-speech, sound muting, link highlighting, and improved content discoverability.

WCAG Compliance

DGA 5.2.2 aligned and WCAG 2.1 AA aligned to help organizations meet accessibility standards.

Plug & Play

Lightweight accessibility layer that integrates instantly into any website without redesigning the platform.
